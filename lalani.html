Azim Lalani, MD

Family Medicine

Dr. Azim Lalani is a family medicine doctor in Bradenton, Florida and is affiliated with multiple hospitals in the area, including Blake Medical Center and Manatee Memorial Hospital. He received his medical degree from Jawaharlal Nehru Medical Collegein Wardha, India. He completed his residency at Family Medicine - Doctors Hospital of Michigan, Wayne State University, Pontiac, MI and has been in practice for 9+ years. He is one of 33 doctors at Blake Medical Center where is currently practicing. He was previously at Pinnacle Medical group for the last 9 years. The procedures & conditions he treats are below.
